# Claim Red-Team Protocol

## Purpose

Stress-test synthesized claims before they become scorecard items or deck claims.

## Adversarial Checks

1. Scope challenge: is the claim broader than the wording of the supporting insights?
2. Source diversity challenge: are all sources effectively the same kind of organization, function, or maturity band?
3. Counter-example search: is there transcript material that weakens or narrows the claim?
4. Terminology challenge: is the claim using a cleaner label than the interviewee actually used?
5. Promotion challenge: what would make this unsafe to score or present on a slide today?

## Output Options

- keep as drafted
- narrow claim wording
- split into multiple claims
- move to `contested_claims.yaml`
- block promotion pending more evidence

## Minimum Record

For any material that reaches scorecard or deck review, capture:

- what was challenged
- what evidence survived
- what remained unresolved
