# Role Redesign

## Short Definition

Role redesign captures how human work changes around AI-enabled workflows, including review, exception handling, cross-selling, querying, and higher-value follow-on tasks.

## Current Synthesis

The current evidence supports workflow-specific redirection of effort, not broad role elimination.

## Evidence-Backed Claims

- `clm-oc-role-shifts-redirect-human-work`

## Important Caveats

- Most role-shift examples are mono-source.
- Do not use this claim as evidence of generalized headcount reduction.
