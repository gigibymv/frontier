# Workflow Reuse

## Short Definition

Workflow reuse is how AI-enabled workflows, automations, prompts, plugins, or templates move from local use to broader organizational reuse.

## Current Synthesis

The evidence supports job-owner-led automation and role-specific tools. The earlier marketplace framing was too strong and has been narrowed to local sharing through a plugin repository in one source.

## Evidence-Backed Claims

- `clm-oc-workflow-owners-build-local-automations`

## Important Caveats

- Do not describe the current evidence as a proven marketplace dynamic.
- Reuse remains partially mono-source and should stay below scorecard/deck use.
