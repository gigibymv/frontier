# Agentic Orchestration

## Short Definition

Agentic orchestration is how multi-step AI work is delegated, sequenced, coordinated, checked, and improved across tools, state, and humans.

## Current Synthesis

The current wiki uses orchestration mainly as a talent/readiness signal: engineering roles may shift toward orchestrating agents and evaluating AI-generated work.
