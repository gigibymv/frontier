# Startup vs Enterprise Patterns

## Current Synthesis

The safe framing is startup-skewed versus enterprise-specific, not startup-only versus enterprise-only.

## Startup-Skewed Signals

- role-level tool building and self-service automation;
- agent orchestration as an engineering skill;
- fast workflow-specific role redesign;
- pragmatic permissioning and customer trust gates.

## Enterprise-Specific Signals

- context ownership as a formal governance pattern;
- local plugin repositories or managed reuse channels;
- future-proof role evaluation and structured approval gates;
- governance-heavy implementation forms.

## Evidence-Backed Claims

- `clm-oc-ai-fluency-as-threshold`
- `clm-oc-workflow-owners-build-local-automations`
- `clm-oc-context-ownership-emerging-role`
- `clm-oc-accountability-based-human-control`

## Caveats

- The strongest distinction is often implementation style, not intrinsic capability.
- Avoid claiming exclusivity unless the corpus proves it.
