# Platform vs Operating Capabilities

## Working Boundary

Platform is the system layer that enables AI-enabled work. Operating Capabilities are the organizational behaviors and governance patterns that make that system useful, safe, and value-producing.

## Platform Examples

- context assembly and freshness mechanisms;
- orchestration and workflow execution;
- permissioned tool access;
- runtime and deployment rails;
- validation and telemetry surfaces.

## Operating Capabilities Examples

- context ownership and approval responsibilities;
- performance expectations for AI fluency;
- human accountability boundaries;
- peer learning and workflow reuse;
- budget gates and experimentation governance.

## Caveat

The same phenomenon can have a Platform side and an Operating Capabilities side. For example, permissioning is a Platform mechanism, while deciding who may use what and when is an Operating Capability.
