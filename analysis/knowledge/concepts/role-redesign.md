# Role Redesign

## Definition

Role redesign captures how human work changes around AI-enabled workflows, including review, exception handling, cross-selling, querying, and higher-value follow-on tasks.

## Evidence Summary

- The current claim layer supports role redesign only as workflow-specific redirection of effort.
- It does not support a broad role-elimination or headcount-reduction claim.

## Open Questions

- Which role shifts are durable versus temporary transition states?
- How should the benchmark distinguish time savings from changed responsibility?
- What evidence is needed before role redesign becomes a scorecard item?
